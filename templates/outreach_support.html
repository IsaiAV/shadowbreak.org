Outreach & Survivor Support

Our Mission

The Shadowbreak Project is dedicated to fighting human trafficking through awareness, education, and direct action.

We work at the intersection of technology, intelligence gathering, and community outreach to identify, prevent, and respond to human trafficking across the globe. Our Shadow Systems Theory framework gives us a unique lens through which to understand and disrupt trafficking networks.

Awareness Campaigns

Exposing The Darkness

Our flagship awareness campaign focuses on bringing to light the hidden realities of human trafficking. Through social media, podcasts, and community events, we dismantle misconceptions and educate the public on recognizing and reporting suspected trafficking.

Join our exclusive X (Twitter) community: Exposing The Darkness

Listen to our podcast on: Spotify

Community Workshops

We conduct regular workshops for schools, universities, law enforcement, healthcare providers, and community organizations on:

  • Recognizing signs of trafficking
  • Understanding trafficking networks
  • Safely reporting suspicious activity
  • Supporting survivors
  • Using technology to combat trafficking

To request a workshop for your organization, please contact us.

Survivor Support

Our Approach

The Shadowbreak Project bridges the gap between survivors and the resources they need. Our trauma-informed approach recognizes the unique challenges faced by trafficking survivors, and we connect individuals with appropriate services based on their specific needs.

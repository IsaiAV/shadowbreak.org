Volunteer with the Shadowbreak Project

Join Our Mission

The Shadowbreak Project is powered by dedicated volunteers committed to fighting human trafficking and making a difference in the lives of survivors.

Our volunteers come from diverse backgrounds, each bringing unique skills and perspectives to our work. Whether you have a few hours a month or want to become deeply involved, there's a place for you in our community.

Volunteer Opportunities

OSINT Analysts

Help gather and analyze open-source intelligence to identify patterns and trends related to human trafficking.

Skills Needed:
  • Research and analytical abilities
  • Attention to detail
  • Experience with data analysis (preferred)
  • Understanding of OSINT principles (can be trained)

Training in Shadow Systems Theory and the Field Distortion Engine will be provided.

Technology Volunteers

Contribute to the development and maintenance of our digital tools, including the Field Distortion Engine and other OSINT platforms.

Skills Needed:
  • Software development
  • Data science or machine learning
  • Web development
  • Cybersecurity

Familiarity with Python, NLP, or data visualization is particularly valuable.

Outreach & Education

Help spread awareness about human trafficking and the work of the Shadowbreak Project.

Skills Needed:
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • Experience in education or training
  • Content creation
  • Event planning

Opportunities include leading workshops, creating educational materials, and representing the organization at events.

Social Media & Communications

Help manage our social media presence and create content that educates and inspires action.

Skills Needed:
  • Social media management
  • Content creation
  • Graphic design
  • Writing and editing

Experience with platforms like X (Twitter), Instagram, Facebook, and Spotify is valuable.

Volunteer Requirements

General Requirements

  • 18 years of age or older
  • Commitment to the mission and values of the Shadowbreak Project
  • Ability to maintain confidentiality and handle sensitive information ethically
  • Reliable internet access (for remote volunteers)
  • Commitment of at least 3-5 hours per month (varies by role)

Background Check

Due to the sensitive nature of our work, all volunteers must undergo a background check. This helps ensure the safety of everyone involved, especially survivors.

Training

All volunteers receive comprehensive training, including:

  • Introduction to the Shadowbreak Project
  • Understanding human trafficking
  • Trauma-informed approaches
  • Role-specific training
  • Shadow Systems Theory (as applicable)

Application Process

How to Apply

  1. Complete the volunteer interest form by contacting us
  2. Participate in an initial interview (virtual or in-person)
  3. Complete the background check process
  4. Attend orientation and training sessions
  5. Begin volunteering!
Ready to Join?

To express your interest in volunteering, please contact us at team@shadowbreakproject.org with the subject line "Volunteer Interest" or visit our Contact page.
